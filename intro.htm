About Tan Song Yun

Profile

Tan Song Yun is a Chinese mainland actress and singer who was born in Luzhou, Sichuan, China. Her real name is Tan Jing Jing and she is also known as Seven Tan. She was born on 31 May 1990 and her Chinese zodiac is a horse. Since she was young, she likes reading and dancing. So in 2001, she went to Sichuan Dance School to learn classical dance and folk dance. She was a child star and was graduated from Beijing Film Academy.

Early Life

Tan Songyun has been exposed to performing since she was fifteen years old. She was particularly talented in art when she was a child, and she was also very conspicuous when she was with other children. But when she decided to repeat her National College Entrance Examination which takes a year, to enroll to the Beijing Film Academy, no one is optimistic about her including her relatives and friends. Fortunately, although her family's financial burden was great, her parents are very supportive of her decision. She also lived up to her parents trust and successfully enrolled to the Beijing Film Academy.

Discography

Tan Song Yun is not only an actress but also a singer. She has sung songs for many of her TV series and online dramas. For example, the With You OST "Dearest Classmate", the Under Power OST "This Summer" and the Go Ahead OST "Like a Breeze". Other than that, Tan Song Yun also has her own original single song which named "I'm here" and this songs was fully written and composed on her own. She wants people who listen to this song to know that there will always be someone beside them and they are also loved.

Career

Tan Song Yun started shooting TV series and become a child actress in 2005. The first TV series that allowed her to enter the audience's field of vision and receive the audience's attention was the Empresses in the Palace. But she did not become popular because of it, she was unknown for 5 years. Finally in 2016, she starred in the dramas With You and The Whirlwind Girl which let her became famous. Since then, she has acted in many TV series of the same type and achieved good results. Which includes Faceoff, My Mr.Mermaid, The Fox's Summer and The Whirlwind Girl 2. Unfortunately, this left a stereotype to the audience and a lot of negative labels were put on her. Therefore in 2017, Tan Song Yun hone hour performing skills in the show The Birth of an Actor. In the end, she refreshed the audience’s original impression of her with the TV series Under The Power.
